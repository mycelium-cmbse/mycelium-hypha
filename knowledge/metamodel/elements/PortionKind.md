# PortionKind

`Occurrences` package · enumeration

PortionKind is an enumeration of the specific kinds of Occurrence portions that can be represented by an OccurrenceUsage.

## Literals

- `timeslice` — A time slice of an Occurrence (a portion over time).
- `snapshot` — A snapshot of an Occurrence (a time slice with zero duration).
